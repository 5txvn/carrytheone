# Converting Base 2 to Base 8
## Notes
+ Right-to-left trick
## General Explanation
1. Start with the last three digits. Convert them from base 2 to base 10. Write this result on the right side of the answer blank.
2. Move to the next three digits, and repeat the same process until there are no more digits to move to.
## Example 1
$$11001010_2=\_\_\_\__8$$
1. Take the last three digits (010), and convert them from base 2 to base 10 ($010_2=2$). Write down this result on the right (**2**)
2. Move to the next three (001), and convert again ($001_2=1$). Write down this result to the left of the 2 (**12**)
3. Move to the next/last two (11), and convert again ($11_2=3$). Write down this result to the left of the 12 (**312**)
4. Final Answer: **312**
## Example 2
$$110111_2=\_\_\_\__8$$
1. Take the last three digits (111), and convert them from base 2 to base 10 ($111_2=7$). Write down this result on the right (**7**)
2. Move to the next/last three (110), and convert again ($110_2=6$). Write down this result to the left of the 6 (**7**)
4. Final Answer: **67**
