# Remainder When Dividing By 3
## Notes
None.
## General Explanation
1. Sum up all the digits of the number you're dividing
2. Divide the sum by 3 and write the remainder
## Example 1
*What is the remainder of $629\div 3$?*
1. Sum up all the digits of 629 to get $6+2+9=17$
2. Divide 17 by 3 to get a remainder of 2
3. Final Answer: **2**
## Example 2
*What is the remainder of $51493\div 3$?*
1. Sum up all the digits of 51493 to get $5+1+4+9+3=22$
2. Divide 22 by 3 to get a remainder of 1
3. Final Answer: **1**
