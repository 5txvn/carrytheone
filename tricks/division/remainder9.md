# Remainder When Dividing By 9
## Notes
None.
## General Explanation
This trick is the exact same as remainder when dividing by 3 except you divide the sum by 9 now.
## Example 1
*What is the remainder of $629\div 9$?*
1. Sum up all the digits of 629 to get $6+2+9=17$
2. Divide 17 by 9 to get a remainder of 8
3. Final Answer: **8**
## Example 2
*What is the remainder of $51493\div 3$?*
1. Sum up all the digits of 51493 to get $5+1+4+9+3=22$
2. Divide 22 by 9 to get a remainder of 4
3. Final Answer: **4**
