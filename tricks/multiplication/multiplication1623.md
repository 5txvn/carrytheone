# Multiplication by $16\frac 23$
## Notes
+ Number must be divisible by 6
## General Explanation
If you know a few decimal forms of common fractions, it shouldn't be difficult to recognize that $16\frac 23=16.666\dots=\frac{100}6$. Thus, we are able to leverage this fact to multiply any number divisible by 6 by $16\frac 23$ by doing the following:<br>
1. Divide the number by 6
2. Multiply by 100
## Example 1
$$36\times 16\frac 23=$$
1. $36\div 6=6$
2. $6\times 100=600$
3. Final Answer: **600**
## Example 2
$$84\times 16\frac 23=$$
1. $84\div 6=14$
2. $14\times 100=1400$
3. Final Answer: **1400**
