# Double Integral of xy
## Notes
None.
## General Explanation
Given the problem is in the form $\int_a^b \int_c^d xy\mathrm{dxdy}$, use the formula $\frac{(b^2-a^2)(d^2-c^2)}4$ to solve the integral.
## Example 1
$$\int_2^3 \int_4^5 xy\mathrm{dxdy}$$
1. The answer is $\frac{(3^2-2^2)(5^2-4^2)}4=\frac{45}4$
